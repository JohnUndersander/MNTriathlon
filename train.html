Training/Membership Requirements

Training with the MN Tri Team can be as intense or laid back as you want to make it. We offer a number of coached practices at different times throughout the week so that members can choose convenient practices that fit their schedules. Check out the requirements for our non-racing members and racing members below!


Non-Racing Members
  • Come to any amount of practices that fits your schedule!
Racing Members
  • Attend at least 3 practices per week
  • Receive extra coaching attention, and represent our team at MWCTC races and Collegiate Nationals

NOTE: Prospective members can attend up to 3 practices without paying a team fee, so don't hesitate to drop in on one of our practices!
