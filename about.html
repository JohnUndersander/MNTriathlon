Do you like to swim, bike, or run? Find training partners here!

The Minnesota Triathlon Team's purpose is to provide a fun, healthy, and structured atmosphere for students to pursue their fitness and triathlon goals. We offer many coached practices a week for individuals to work out together, including swimming, biking, running, and strength training. University of Minnesota students and staff of any level and experience are welcome to join us!

Members that choose to race for the team participate in the Midwest Collegiate Triathlon Conference races throughout the year. We also send a team to the USA Triathlon Collegiate National Championships held annually in late April.

As a team we receive great discounts on Collegiate Conference races. For away races we travel as a team to minimize costs.

Please contact us at minntri@umn.edu with any questions about the team and joining.
