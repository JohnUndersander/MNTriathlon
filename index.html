Welcome to the homepage of the Minnesota Triathlon Team! We are a University of Minnesota student group that aims to provide a fun and healthy environment to train for triathlons and other multisport events! We offer numerous practices each week for members, including swimming, biking, running, and strength training. We welcome all ability and experience levels.

Please contact us at minntri@umn.edu with any comments or questions.
