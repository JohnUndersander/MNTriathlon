President - Will Haltom

Will is a senior from St. Cloud, Minnesota. He is a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics, and this is second year on the exec board of MN Triathlon. He has been a lifelong swimmer and runner, but is relatively new to the sport of triathlon, having joined as a Sophomore after spending a year on the rowing team. He has greatly enjoyed his time on the team so far, particularly team trips and competing in Collegiate Nationals in 2015 and 2016. He is looking forward to further developing MN Triathlon into a competitive, fun, and successful multi-sport team.

Co-Vice President - Melanie Rabino

Melanie is a first year pharmacy school student from Woodbury, MN. She just graduated from the U of M with a degree in Chemistry. This will be her 5th year on the team and 4th year on the exec board. Melanie has been a competitive swimmer since age 7 and has been racing triathlons since high school. She loves the sport of triathlon and especially this team since it has allowed her to train for the sport she enjoys so much in a supportive and fun environment. She really cherishes the friendships she has made through this team. Melanie is excited for her first year on the team as a graduate student and hopes to help the team grow, get faster, and have fun.

Treasurer - Miranda Olson

Miranda is a senior from Detroit Lakes, Minnesota majoring in Art. She first started competing in triathlons with her family as a fun and healthy way to get together in the summers. Having swam and ran competitively throughout middle school and high school she found that she had a solid foundation for the sport and enjoyed the challenge it presented. Miranda joined the MN Triathlon team mostly looking for a solid group of people to work out with but soon found herself competing at Collegiate Nationals in Arizona. She has enjoyed pushing herself and becoming friends with a great group of people along the way. She looks forward to the coming year with the team and improving her race!

Co-Vice President - Hannah Eldridge

Hannah is a senior from Madison, WI majoring in Speech-Language-Hearing Sciences and minoring in Spanish and Psychology. Having dabbled in swimming and triathlon throughout her youth, she looked forward to joining the triathlon team at the University of Minnesota as soon as she started her freshman year. Hannah enjoys having the team as a consistent group to train with and keep her committed to working hard in preparation for races. She has competed with the team at Collegiate Nationals the past two years and looks forward to the races ahead. Hannah is excited for her second year on the exec board and hopes to see the team grow and improve in new ways this school year.

Coach - Louis Hughes

Louis Hughes has been participating in duathlons, aquathons, and, of course, triathlons for nearly 12 years. He competed in Track and Cross Country in his Middle and High School years, and was taught to swim by his Track/CC/Swimming Coach Joe Wildman. He excelled in Track and Field, and continued to run for the University of Minnesota-Duluth where he became a school record holder in multiple mid-distance events. Although he spent most days during his collegiate years running, he also raced in many Swimming and Biking events. He is currently a member of Team TriScottsdale, is a 2-time USAT All-American, and an ITU World Championship Qualifier. This is his first year coaching the Minnesota Triathlon Team, and he is very excited to see the athletes develop new skills and have their most successful season yet.
